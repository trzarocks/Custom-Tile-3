Our Story

Founded in 1989, Custom Tile & Marble, Inc. is a family-owned and operated business based in Parsonsburg, Maryland. For over 35 years, we have been transforming homes and businesses across Maryland, Delaware, and Virginia with our expertise in tile and natural stone. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and personalized service has made us a trusted name on the Eastern Shore. From our expansive showroom and warehouse, we offer a wide selection of granite, marble, quartz, and ceramic tiles, along with custom fabrication and installation services. Whether it’s a luxurious marble countertop, a stunning tile shower, or durable flooring, our team brings precision and passion to every project.
